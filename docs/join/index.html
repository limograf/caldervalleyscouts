Calder Valley Scouts

Join 8th/26th Calder Valley Scouts

Jump in and get muddy. Give back and get set. Scouts ignore the butterflies and go for it, and soon so will you.

Key Info

Beavers are for boys and girls from 6 to 8 years old, and meet from 5.30pm to 6.45pm every Friday

Cubs are for boys and girls from 8 to 11 years old, and meet from 6.00pm to 7.15pm every Thursday.

Scouts are for boys and girls from 11 to 14 years old, and meet from 7.30pm to 9.15pm every Thursday.

Explorers are for boys and girls from 14 to 18 years old, and meet from 7.30pm to 9.15pm every Thursday.

Join us

If you would like to come and see what we do then please come along to any of the meetings. All meetings start at Central Methodist Church, Todmorden, just off Bramsche Square. If you want to find out any more details please e mail dave@caldervalleyscouts.org.uk or call 08432890163 or find us on Facebook
